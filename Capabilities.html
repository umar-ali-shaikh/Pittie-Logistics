Transport Management System (TMS)

The TMS platform enables collaborative logistics management. It enables collaboration with customers, BAs and partners. It helps save operational and freight costs through shipment consolidation. The platform provides 360 degree transparency through data sharing across all stakeholders. It provides seamless visibility to operations resulting in operational efficiency as well as provide analytics and MIS reports

Control Tower

Pittie Logistics core strength lies in our centralized control tower. Facilitated by technology & highly skilled workforce, we are able to add value to our customer's supply chain. We have designed extensive dashboards and reports which help us monitor and drive productivity, TAT, resolve escalation & ensure customer satisfaction. Our advance data analytics helps us in better business forecast, risk mitigation and efficient decision making.

Warehouse Management System (WMS)

Pittie logistics’ network of warehouses is managed through our Warehouse Management System (WMS) designed to support and optimise warehouse functionality and distribution centre management. This enables efficient & cost effective management of your inventory and fulfilment processes.

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

Pittie Logistics uses Microsoft’s Dynamics 365 Business Central ERP designed to connect the diverse dynamics of our business. By utilizing a robust data base, such as Microsoft Power BI, business data can be generated and shared for analysis across the enterprise. The role tailored interface has 21 “out of the box” roles customizable to adapt & manage our unique business needs.
