Carry & Forward Partners

Ideally recommended for new brands to partner with us and utilize our widespread network of warehouses & supplies across India. We build the business model considering the early life cycle of the brand, thus letting it focus its margin investments on sales growth as well as ensuring the achievement of distribution KPIs. A win-win long-term sustainable model.

Superstockist

Pittie brings in the financial appetite to cater to the growth of its partner brands. A brand's developing sales momentum calls for strategic financial holdings in inventory to keep the working capital steady and achieve scale. Our team makes this complex model easy by recommending precise stock placement against observed sales & rolling forecasts, operations, fulfilling SLA's in your value chain, and much more.

Built To Suit

The dynamics of the market demand intelligent customized business models that require a skilled task force to analyze our partners' needs and design tailor-made business models. Our robust capabilities ensure the seamless execution of bespoke solutions with utmost efficiency. We focus on designing and discovering standard operating procedures for our clients to implement, closely measure, and continually improvise to optimize their personalized business needs and costs.
