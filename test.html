Transport Management System (TMS)

Pittie Logistic core strength lies in its centralized Control Tower. Facilitated by technology and a highly-skilled workforce that manages the core network of 19+ DCs across India. We have designed extensive dashboards and reports that help us monitor and drive productivity, shelf-life tracking, delivery time, reduce wastages, and resolve escalation. We ensure customer satisfaction with our advanced data analytics, which helps us in performance analysis, business forecasts, risk mitigation, and efficient decision making.
