About Pittie Logistics

Pittie Logistics is a leading third-party logistics (3PL) provider in India, delivering end-to-end solutions from “storage to stores” across warehousing, transportation, and value-added services. Part of the Pittie Group (est. 1991), we combine decades of supply-chain expertise with advanced technology, a 1000+ strong workforce, and a strong national and global presence to deliver customized, data-driven logistics solutions that drive efficiency and scalable growth.

Mr. Aditya Pittie

Managing Director, Pittie Group

Mr. Aditya Pittie, Managing Director of Pittie Group, represents a new generation of dynamic Indian entrepreneurs who are reshaping traditional business models with vision, innovation, and agility. Under his leadership, the Pittie Group has evolved into a diversified conglomerate with strong footholds in consumer products, logistics, and media. An alumnus of King’s College, London, where he earned a degree in Management & Leadership, Aditya’s global perspective and sharp business acumen have laid the foundation for the Group’s transformation. His leadership is marked by a focus on sustainable growth, operational excellence, and an unwavering commitment to quality. At the heart of the Group’s diversification is Pittie Consumer, which has emerged as a fast-growing force in India’s FMCG sector. Guided by Aditya’s consumer-first vision, the division has successfully created trusted brands that blend heritage, innovation, and everyday relevance. From spiritual and devotional essentials that bring authenticity to daily worship to home cleaning and care solutions that elevate household hygiene standards, Pittie Consumer’s portfolio reflects a deep understanding of Indian consumers and their evolving needs. Supporting this growth is the Group’s robust logistics division, which Aditya has strengthened into a nationwide supply chain network with modern warehousing and a presence across 700+ pin codes and 3,500+ touchpoints. This integration of manufacturing, branding, and logistics ensures quality, consistency, and efficiency across every business vertical. Driven by strategic innovation, a culture of excellence, and a deep sense of purpose, Aditya Pittie continues to steer the Pittie Group toward sustainable and consumer-centric growth, reinforcing its reputation as a forward-thinking and values-driven group.
